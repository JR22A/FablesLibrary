In Zootopia, there was an empire of wolves who were led by Alpha. Alpha is a controlling, narcissist, and doesn’t listen to any type of leader and every wolf in his pack is scared of him. He always prioritizes himself and always orders his pack to hunt and bring all his needs without thinking about his pack.

One day, during their hunting season, the pack of Alpha found a lost dog along their way and brought him to their leader. The dog was named Eli and he was a loving, caring, and charming dog.






“Team leader Alpha, we found a dog. What should we do to him?” said one of his wolves. Alpha replied, “Bring him to our territory and train him. He can be useful and be our servant.” The wolves followed the orders given by Alpha.

However, during the staying of Eli with the wolves, Alpha noticed that his pack was getting too close to the dog and found it as a threat in his empire. Alpha became angry and ordered to kill Eli immediately.

“That dog is a threat to our pack! We must exterminate him!” Alpha said angrily. Alpha’s pack didn’t even move and they were too stunted with their leader’s order.

Eli notices that the wolves are slowly stepping back and he builds up his courage to face Alpha and voice out his insights. “Stop this! This is unfair! Your pack is too tired and scared of you. You never listen to them and you still think you are a good leader?!” Eli yelled.

“I don’t care! Wolves, do as I order. Now!” Alpha replied. The wolves did not move once more and just stared at their leader.

“Fine! I will do it myself” Alpha yelled angrily and attacked Eli directly. With the unity of the wolves and Eli, the leader was defeated in the battle.

“If you are always thinking about yourself, these wolves don't deserve you to be their leader! You must leave!”

Alpha decided to leave the empire and Eli became the wolf’s leader and they live happily and got away with unfair treatment.
